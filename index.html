Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

The Grym Tracker app was created by JustTolerable as a free application. This privacy policy explains how user data is handled.

Data Collection

Grym Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data entered by the user (workouts, plans, notes) is stored exclusively locally on the device.

Data Stored Locally

The app saves the following on the user's device:

  • Workout and exercise history
  • Training plans
  • App settings

This data is not transmitted outside the device in any way.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any external analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

If the privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page.
